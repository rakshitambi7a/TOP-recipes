Tun Tun Mosi's Ladoo

Every desi kid's ultimate snack (atleast in their imagination). This is a proper mixture of what a cartoon can do and what a kid can imagine.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Besan/Gram Flour
  • ¼ Cup Ghee
  • ½ Cup Powdered Sugar
  • ½ tsp Cardamom Powder
  • 10 Raisins
  • 1 Almond

Recipe

  1. Dry roast the besan on medium flame for about 10-15mins, keep stirring. Its raw taste should not be there.
  2. Add ghee and mix well.
  3. Cook the besan till fat separates for about 8-10 minutes and turn off the flame.
  4. Add sugar and cardamom powder. Mix well so that there are no lumps.. Let the mixture cool completely.
  5. Grease your hands with ghee and start forming ladoos. Make smooth and round ladoos. Decorate with a raisin.
  6. Serve or store in air tight container.
