Doracake

Every desi kid's ultimate snack (atleast in their imagination). This is a proper mixture of what a cartoon can do and what a kid can imagine.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Refined flour , मैदा
  • ¾ cup Powdered sugar
  • 3 tbsp Condensed milk , कंडेंस्ड मिल्क
  • ½ tsp Baking powder , बेकिंग पाउडर
  • 1 tsp Honey , शहद
  • 1 tsp Vanilla essence , वनीला एसेंस
  • ½ cup Milk , दूध
  • 1 tbsp Butter , मक्खन

Recipe

  1. In a bowl add refined flour, powdered sugar, condensed milk, baking powder, honey, vanilla essence, milk, butter and mix well and make a batter.
  2. Now heat a non-stick pan and a ladle full of the batter and cook it until golden brown then flip it on to the other side and cook that as well.
  3. Repeat this with the rest of the batter. And let them cool down a little.
  4. Then take on cake and spread the hazelnut chocolate spread on it then place another cake on it and press a little.
  5. Serving immediately and enjoy it.
